Bio

I am a senior software engineer at Software R&D Center, Samsung Electronics. At Samsung Electronics, I have been working on mobile technologies, in particular, Web platform for mobile and wearable devices. My research interests are in Web technologies, data management, and query optimization. I obtained my B.Eng in Software Engineering and Ph.D in Computer Science both from The University of New South Wales in 2005 and 2010, respectively. Before joining Samsung Electronics in 2013, I was a postdoctoral researcher at KAIST.
