I am a principal software engineer at Samsung Research, Samsung Electronics. At Samsung Research, I have been working on mobile technologies, in particular, a Web platform for mobile and wearable devices. My research interests are in Web technologies, data management, and query optimization. I obtained my B.Eng in Software Engineering and Ph.D in Computer Science both from The University of New South Wales in 2005 and 2010, respectively. Before joining Samsung Research (previously known as Software R&D Center) in 2013, I was a postdoctoral researcher at KAIST.

Research Interests

Web technology and data management. In particular,

  • Web browser design and optimization
  • Web languages
  • Mobile Web technology
  • Data management and query optimization
